Who is Bruno Mars?

Biography (as of 2022)

Full Name: Peter Gene Hernandez
Birth Place: Hawaii
Artist Name: Bruno Mars
Age: 36
Height: 165cm
Ethnicity: Puerto Rican, Ashkenazi Jewish, Filipino
Partner: Jessica Caban
Instruments played: Piano, bass, drums, guitar, voice
Music styles: Pop, R&B, funk, soul, reggae, disco, rock

Peter Gene Hernandez, known professionally as Bruno Mars, is an American singer, songwriter, record producer, musician, and dancer. He is known for his stage performances, retro showmanship, and for performing in a wide range of musical styles, including pop, R&B, funk, soul, reggae, disco, and rock. Bruno Mars is accompanied by his band, the Hooligans, who play a variety of instruments, such as electric guitar, bass, piano, keyboards, drums, and horns, and also serve as backup singers and dancers.

Achievements

Bruno Mars has won 19 awards in his career, including 11 Grammy Awards.

Bruno Mars has sold over 200 million singles and 26 million albums worldwide, making him one of the best-selling artists of all time.

As of 2019, Bruno Mars has released seven number-one singles on the Billboard Hot 100 since his career launched in 2010, attaining his first five number-one singles faster than any male artist since Elvis Presley.

Bruno Mars’s most famous songs include “Marry You”, “That’s What I like”, “Locked Out Of Heaven”, “Grenade”, “Just the Way You Are”, “24K Magic”, and “Uptown Funk”.

Bruno Mars’s top artist collaborations include Mark Ronson, CeeLo Green, Adele, Travie McCoy, B.o.B, Alicia Keys, Jay-Z, Kanye West, Justin Bieber and Flo Rida.

Lyrics

This song is about Bruno Mars’ “rapturous feelings brought about by a relationship infused with positive emotion”, as described by Genius Lyrics. I also think this song is based on Bruno Mars’ love for Jessica Caban, who has been with him for 11 years. She is known to be the inspiration behind many of Bruno Mars’ love songs.

Melody

The song’s hook is the build-up from “And it shows, yeah, yeah, yeah” to “‘Cause you make me feel like I’ve been locked out of heaven”. The intro, compositing of the bass guitar, drums, and Bruno Mars singing “oh, yeah, yeah” is very catchy and a great start to the song, which is repeated multiple times throughout the song.

Harmony

D minor

Texture/Dynamics

The arrangement builds from the bridge to what we think will lead up to the chorus, but the suspense lasts and leads into the intro, with the bass guitar and catchy drums.
The dynamics stay roughly the same throughout the whole song, at an approximate mezzo forte.


Bruno Mars’ clothing style

Many people find Bruno Mars’ clothing style quite classy and unique. His most popularly worn pieces are silky track jackets, sequin jackets, tuxedos, bold chains, sunglasses and a black hat.

Bruno Mars launched a clothing line with Lacoste x Ricky Regal, on March 5th, 2021. Bruno Mars created this clothing line to celebrate Ricky Regal, his alter ego, who embraces freedom, luxury, and creativity with an effortless wave of luxe. Bruno Mars collaborated with Lacoste to make his dream come to life.

We are offered a sense of Bruno Mars' alter ego through this new collection, since Ricky Regal is almost like an ideal person Bruno Mars would like to be. We see glimpses of personality and individuality, which reflects on Bruno Mars’ inner values.

My Opinionis on His Music

I think that Bruno Mars’ music is very catchy, and the lyrics are quite meaningful and inspiring. His music echoes the great hits of the 1980s, incorporating artistic and musical elements of many famous singers such as Michael Jackson, James Brown, Keith Sweat, Jodeci, and R. Kelly.

I view Bruno Mars as one of the most talented artists in the music industry, since he writes nearly all of his songs (he has written countless songs for other artists, too) and has an amazing, unique voice. Furthermore, at the young age of four, Bruno Mars began performing five days a week with his family's band, The Love Notes, and became known in Hawaii for his impersonation of Elvis Presley.

This shows that he has been working hard for his song writing and singing skills since a very young age, and never fails to disappoint with countless hit songs and albums!

Bruno Mars is rather unlike most other popular artists (e.g Ariana Grande, Queen, Billie Eilish). This is because Bruno Mars’ music is deeper that most and isn’t just about a catchy beat. His music is known to have inspired and touched others, allowing them to connect to his songs on a deeper level. I do, however, understand that some of his songs aren’t like that, and serve a purpose to “make people dance”, as Bruno Mars once stated, in one of his interviews. However, he is a very skilled and talented songwriter, and he always finds creative ways to impress his audience with his singing too.
